## The Competitor

[[[Ken Broadway]]]

## Writeup

This deck tech on runner up for GenCon World Championship 2018.  Originally
posted: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7ABFM9rWAaY&list=PLTvAO_RppJ8WbNDWHxHdG-x017Zyk2248&index=1

Notably this deck is quite old, but still relevant tidbits that make it useful.
The biggest piece of advice is not to overuse the Gimmick and it becomes really
powerful in the right moment.
