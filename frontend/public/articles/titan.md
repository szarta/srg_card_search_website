## The Competitor

[[[Titan]]]

## Writeup

This deck tech originally posted:  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MNQzE3pq2k4&list=PLTvAO_RppJ8WbNDWHxHdG-x017Zyk2248&index=4

The 1-3 are not Lead stops because the Gimmick can blank a Lead.  [[Rolling
Headlock]] and [[Jump Kick]] are on the lowest skills, so there's a high chance
to get them back when you are losing the turn, to be able to discard for
Gimmick.  [[Scoop Slam]] to shuffle cards back.
